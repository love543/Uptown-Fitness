What goes into building our trainer team

We Set Our Standards High
‍‍We don’t just rigorously screen trainers before they join our team — we set the standard for what it means to be a top personal trainer in today’s connected world with our rigorous vetting process, proprietary online personal training curriculum, and ongoing quality assurance for client service.
Having high standards is how we built a remarkable and diverse team of trainers who are deeply invested in and incentivized by your success, and why 95% of existing clients gave their personal trainer a 5-star review.

We Require a National Certification
‍Every one of our trainers is nationally certified through ACE, NSCA, ACSM or NASM, which means they have completed at least 100 hours of rigorous study and application of exercise science, nutrition, and anatomical subject matter. Because every trainer also has a bachelor’s degree (at minimum), multiple years of experience training clients in-person, and at least one additional wellness-related credential to enhance their expertise, you can be confident you’ll be paired with a highly qualified, knowledgeable, and reliable expert to partner with you on your goals.

We Build a Diverse Team With Various Specialties
‍Many trainers also have specializations to enhance their coaching, including but not limited to: Powerlifting • Bodybuilding • Athletic Conditioning • Performance Psychology • Nutrition • Stress Management • Behavioral Science • Ayurveda • Metabolic Conditioning • Running • Yoga • Swimming • Booty Building • HIIT • Kettlebell Training • Core Strength • Weight Loss • Muscle Building • Pre & Post Natal Fitness • Corrective Exercise & More
